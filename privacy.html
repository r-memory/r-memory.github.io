Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 8, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information collected through the R-memory Android application.

2. No Data Collection from the App Itself

The R-memory Android application does not collect any personal information from users directly. This includes but is not limited to:

  • User data: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.
  • Device information: Device identifiers, location data, or usage data.
  • App usage: Information about how you use the app, such as the features you access or the time spent within the app.

3. Potential Data Collection by Google APIs

If the R-memory Android application utilizes Google APIs, it is important to note that Google may collect data related to your interactions with those APIs. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on their data collection practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-US

4. No Network Connectivity

The R-memory Android application is designed to function offline and does not require a network connection. This ensures that no data is transmitted to or from the app, except for potential data collection by Google APIs as described above.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by updating the Effective Date at the beginning of this policy.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the R-memory Android application, please contact us at rmemory.app@gmail.com.
